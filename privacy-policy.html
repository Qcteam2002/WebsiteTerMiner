Privacy Policy

Effective starting: 16.11.2024

1. Introduction

Thank you for using TikMiner. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension. TikMiner helps users download videos from TikTok, YouTube, and various e-commerce platforms like AliExpress, Amazon, Shopee, and Temu.

2. Information We Collect

Our extension may collect the following types of information:

  • Browsing Information: To identify compatible videos on e-commerce product pages, TikMiner may read the current URL and detect page content related to the platforms we support (e.g., AliExpress, Shopee).
  • Storage of User Preferences: We may store certain user preferences locally on your device to improve functionality, such as the format or location preferences for downloaded files.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to provide and improve the functionality of the extension, such as detecting relevant content on supported websites and facilitating the download of videos. We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) from users.

4. Data Security

We take data security seriously. The TikMiner extension only accesses the minimum data required for its functionality and does not transmit any data to external servers. All operations, including detection of video content and downloads, occur locally on your device.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not share, sell, or distribute any information collected by TikMiner with third parties. As there is no server-side data collection, there is no data sharing beyond your local device.

6. Permissions Required

Our extension requests specific permissions solely to fulfill its functionalities:

  • Tabs and Active Tab Permissions: To detect and interact with supported websites for identifying downloadable video content.
  • Storage Permission: To save user preferences on your device.
  • Downloads Permission: To allow the extension to download videos selected by the user.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify users by updating the effective date at the top of this document. We encourage users to review this page periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the practices of our extension, please contact us at tikminer.info@gmail.com..
